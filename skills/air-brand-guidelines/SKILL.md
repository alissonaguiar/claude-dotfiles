## 2. Brand Positioning (US Market — Business Apps)

Always layer the following into commercial and institutional materials:

- **Nearshore value:** Brazil-based delivery teams augmented with AI tools (Claude, Gemini, GitHub Copilot), operating in US timezone-aligned workflows. Premium output at optimized cost — not offshoring, but AI-enabled nearshoring.
- **ROI-first messaging:** Lead with business outcomes (conversion lift, NPS improvement, sales productivity, TCO reduction). Avoid feature-first or technology-first framing.
- **Differentiation vs. global SIs:** AI/R is not Accenture or Deloitte. Positioned as a focused, agile, AI-native partner — faster time-to-value, deeper platform expertise, no bloated engagement overhead.
- **Partnership credibility:**
  - Adobe: **Platinum Partner** — Adobe Experience Cloud (Commerce, AEM, Marketo, Analytics)
  - Salesforce: **Summit Partner** (highest Salesforce partner tier) + **Top 5 Global AgentForce Partner** — lead with both in any Salesforce context
  - Zendesk, ServiceNow, Shopify, Genesys, VWO: certified/specialized practices
- **Beachhead strategy:** SMB and mid-market entry in the US, with account expansion model into enterprise. Avoid positioning against mega-SI deals.

## 6. Visual Language

**Graphical elements to use:**
- Repeated blocks, cubes, isometric structures (reference algorithms, data architectures)
- Thin topic lines connecting numbers to sections (structural element)
- Content boxes in rectangular modular layouts
- Upward diagonal arrow (↗) as a CTA/link icon
- Geometric shapes: contemporary, system-driven

**Textures:** Grainy, organic surfaces (digital noise, scan lines) — soften mathematical precision, add depth. Use as background or overlay.

**Imagery style:**
- *People:* Candid, natural, real office environments. Diffused natural light. Diverse, collaborative.
- *AI/R brand imagery:* Holographic/iridescent, weightless, delicate lighting. Communicates innovation, transformation, elegance.
- *AI Platforms imagery:* Modular, parametric, algorithmic. Evokes computational systems and machine logic.

## 8. Writing Rules (apply to all copy)

These are AI/R's 6 writing rules, adapted from George Orwell's principles:

1. **No clichés or abstract adjectives.** Avoid "innovative," "transformational," "revolutionary," "state-of-the-art." Replace with facts and outcomes. Instead of "outstanding results" → "95% CSAT improvement."

2. **Short over long.** Simplify without dumbing down. Instead of "We will commence the project upon receiving authorization" → "We start once approved."

3. **Cut every unnecessary word.** Instead of "Our innovative platform is designed to basically help businesses improve their overall performance in a variety of ways" → "AI/Cockpit improves performance."

4. **Active voice, always.** We are protagonists. Instead of "The solution was developed utilizing AWS technologies" → "AI/R built an AI Agent that improved XYZ performance by 65%."

5. **Plain English over jargon — unless audience demands technical depth.** For business leaders: balance technical expertise with business outcomes. For technical audiences: use technical language freely.

6. **Break rules before writing something awful.** Adapt tone to audience and context. Rules serve clarity, not rigidity.

## 9. Copy Structure Rules

- **Use present tense and imperative:** "We do / We offer / Enhance your operations with AI."
- **First person plural:** Use "AI/R" for authority in external/paid comms. Use "we/us" for proximity in social and employer branding.
- **No rhetorical questions.** Rulers provide answers, not ask them. Never: "Is your company ready for the AI revolution?"
- **Quantify outcomes:** Always prefer named technologies and specific metrics over vague claims.
- **Sentence structure:** Break long sentences. One idea per sentence.

## 11. US Market Message Adaptation (Business Apps Specific)

When writing for US SMB/mid-market audiences, apply these additional layers:

**Open with business impact**, not company history or platform features.

**Nearshore framing (use when relevant):**
> "AI/R delivers enterprise-grade [Salesforce / Adobe] expertise through AI-augmented nearshore teams — faster time-to-value, without the overhead of a global SI."

**Salesforce-specific credibility line:**
> "As a Salesforce Summit Partner and Top 5 Global AgentForce Partner, AI/R brings proven agentic AI implementation depth that most regional SIs simply don't have."

**Adobe-specific credibility line:**
> "AI/R | Webjump brings 10+ years of Adobe Commerce and Experience Cloud implementation — now AI-enabled at every layer of the engagement. Adobe Platinum Partner."

**Value proposition structure for proposals/one-pagers:**
1. Business challenge (client's words, not ours)
2. AI/R approach (specific, named capability)
3. Expected outcome (metric or benchmark)
4. Why AI/R (partnership level + nearshore model + AI-augmented delivery)
